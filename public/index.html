#about us

One of a kind dining

Welcome to Zaytuna, our new Mediterranean restaurant in Bawtry. We’re a small, family-run spot where every dish is made with love, using traditional recipes passed down through generations. We specialize in homemade Syrian classics, like creamy hummus, crispy falafel, and rich lamb shawarma. Our goal is to give you a warm, fast, and friendly place to enjoy fresh, flavorful food that feels like home.
